Help

How the travel advisory works

The app combines your health profile with a local destination guide to estimate travel risk and suggest useful precautions.

1. Create an account

Use the signup page to create a local account, then sign in to access the assessment.

2. Fill your profile

Enter age, height, weight, blood pressure, and any existing health condition so the model can tailor the risk advice.

3. Choose a destination

Enter a destination to view temperature, humidity, AQI, and common disease alerts for that location.

4. Review the result

The result page shows a low-to-critical risk level, environmental data, and travel precautions.
